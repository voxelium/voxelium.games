Kitty Prankster

3D platform game in which the player controls a prankster kitten. The kitten throws cans of soda towards the player's screen. By hitting the screen and various targets, the player scores points and unlocks obstacle systems.
A can of soda is a lot of fun to open and hit various objects funny, but some cans are better to jump over so as not to lose a life in the game.
